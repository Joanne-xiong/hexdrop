About Hex Drop

Hex Drop is an addictive and elegant browser puzzle game inspired by Tetris. Rotate and stack falling hexagons to match colors and clear space. How long can you last before the stack overflows?

Features

  • Smooth gameplay and responsive controls
  • Minimalist visual style
  • Completely free to play
  • Works on desktop and mobile

Controls

Use your keyboard arrow keys to rotate and drop the blocks. On mobile, simply tap the screen to rotate the hexagons.
