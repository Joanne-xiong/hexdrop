About

Hex Drop is a fast-paced puzzle game where you rotate falling hexagons to match colors. The more you match, the higher your score! Compete with friends and train your brain.

Features

  • Fast-paced puzzle gameplay
  • Minimalist graphics, focused fun
  • Play directly in your browser
  • No download required

Controls

  • Left/Right Arrow – Rotate Hexagon
  • Down Arrow – Drop Faster
  • Space – Pause Game
